About Us

The Government of Jharkhand is committed to providing Simple, Accountable, Responsive, and Transparent (SMART) governance to its citizens.

ABOUT

The Government of Jharkhand is committed to provide Simple, Accountable, Responsive and Transparent (SMART) governance to its citizens. In this context, to enable seamless services to the citizens of Jharkhand using Information Technology, Department of IT & e-GoV has decided to create a centralized database of all the citizens of Jharkhand by consolidating their demographic and personal data which are scattered across multiple department databases. This unified database is called as Unified Digital Data Platform or UDDP.

VISION

To provide a strong data platform to the state having largest coverage of unique beneficiaries and families within the state establishing it as one of the most effective, rich, authentic, and single source of beneficiary information leading to multiple benefits for both citizens and the Government. On one hand, it will act as a single platform for citizens of the state where they can not only get the information about the government benefits, they are eligible for, but will also be able to apply for getting those benefits with minimum effort and documentation making their life easy. On the other hand, it will act as an extremely important source of information for the Government enabling data driven decision making & planning for the state ultimately leading towards Proactive Governance.

MISSION

To build a single data platform of beneficiaries for the entire state of Jharkhand having uniquely identified beneficiaries and families using the data from multiple existing beneficiary data sources available with the state by use of latest technologies of deduplication, data quality enhancement and enrichment. One of the purposes is to enable the State Government with a consolidated view of different demographic distribution of beneficiaries along with benefit distribution across families & individuals through effective dashboards created using latest Business Analytics tools and technologies. The ultimate purpose is to make life easy for citizens/ beneficiaries of the state while getting any service/benefit from government by providing a single and easy to use technological platform and providing the platform for proactive governance to the Government.
